Developed an interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product trends, and regional insights using DAX, Power Query, and data visualization techniques.

The dashboard provides valuable business insights through interactive reports, KPI cards, maps, charts, and filters, enabling data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Monitor regional sales trends.
- Track product-wise sales and quantity.
- Compare sales against targets.
- Identify top-performing customers.
- Analyze yearly and monthly sales trends.

## 📋 Business Insights

- East region generated the highest sales.
- Electronics category contributed the largest revenue.
- Mouse and Monitor products achieved the highest sales quantity.
- Sales exceeded the target value significantly.
- Strong growth was observed compared to the previous year.
